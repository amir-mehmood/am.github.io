Biography

Dr.-Ing. Amir Mehmood obtained his Ph.D. degree in Engineering from the department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Technische Universit√§t Berlin / Deutsche Telekom Innovation Laboratories, Berlin, Germany in 2012 under the supervision of Prof. Anja Feldmann. During his Ph.D., he interned with Dr. Walter Willinger (AT&T Research Labs) at the Institute of Pure Mathematics (IPAM), University of California Los Angeles, USA in 2008 and 2011. Prior to that he received degrees of M.Sc. (Electrical Engineering) and B.Sc. (Electrical Engineering) in 2005 and 1996, respectively. After graduation, he has worked at TELIPS Payphones as Manager Technical, Siemens Engineering, Pakistan as Senior Executive Engineer, and Pakistan Telecommunication Company Limited as ADE Network Administrator, Pakistan Internet Exchange. Currently, he is Assistant Professor at Al-Khawarizmi Institute of Computer Science (KICS), University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore.
